ABOUT

"Sporion 3.0 " is an sports event organised in Engineering College Jhalawar held from 19 february to 25 february including various sports like cricket, basketball, volleyball, sprint, chess, carrom, badminton, tug of war, kho-kho and marathon. The word Sporion is made up of two words Sports and Orion. Orion representing a constellation that looks like hunter having four main stars representing coordination, unity, discipline and spirit. All teams were competing for the single title hunter and gave their best & healthy competetion, spirit was maintained during the event sometimes it's not about to win it's all about to experience that you gain. Sports teaches us discipline. The value of time, togetherness and unity.
